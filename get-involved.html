YOUR DONATION HELPS SAVE LIVES IN THE MOUNTAINS.

Every dollar you give directly supports rescue operations, safety education, equipment upgrades, and community outreach. At Kalmont Mountain Rescue, we rely on the generosity of individuals like you to stay prepared, equipped, and ready to respond — no matter the conditions.

Your support funds vital training for volunteers, avalanche safety workshops, emergency response gear, and the day-to-day resources that keep our team on the mountain and in the field. Whether you’re a one-time donor or a recurring supporter, you’re part of every successful rescue and every safe return home.

Help us protect those who explore, work, and live near Kalmont’s rugged terrain. With your contribution, we can continue to make a real difference — one rescue at a time.
